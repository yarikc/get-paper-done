# Audience

## Audience Boundary

This profile describes the reader model, objections, proof standard, desired reader shift, and paper-specific adaptation rules. It does not define interactive TUI behavior, file snapshot policy, feedback approval mechanics, or workflow gates.

## Primary Audience

Platform operating sponsor who decides whether the pilot pattern should continue for another quarter.

## Secondary Audience

Architecture and delivery leads who need to understand what metric changed and what did not.

## Audience Tension

The sponsor wants a concise decision recommendation. Delivery leads need enough metric context to trust that the recommendation is not overclaiming.

## Audience Set

| Audience | Priority | Needs | Likely Objection |
|----------|----------|-------|------------------|
| Operating sponsor | Primary | Decision, metric evidence, risk | One quarter may be too little proof |
| Delivery leads | Secondary | Baseline, sample, scope | The pilot may not generalize |

## Conflict Rule

Decision usefulness wins, but every numerical claim must retain baseline, sample, and timeframe context.
