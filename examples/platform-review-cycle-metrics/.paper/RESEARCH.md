## Research Summary

The synthetic source pack supports two bounded metric claims:

- Median review cycle time moved from 10 days to 7 days across 20 sampled review packets over one quarter. Sources: S1, S2.
- Average rework items moved from 6 to 4 per packet across the same sample. Sources: S1, S3.

The same source pack does not support enterprise ROI, compliance improvement, or broad productivity claims. Source: S4.
