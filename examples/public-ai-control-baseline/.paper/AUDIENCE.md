# Audience

## Audience Boundary

This profile describes the reader model, objections, proof standard, desired reader shift, and paper-specific adaptation rules. It does not define interactive TUI behavior, file snapshot policy, feedback approval mechanics, or workflow gates.

## Primary Audience

Senior technical decision makers who can approve a minimum control baseline for internal generative AI pilots.

## Secondary Audience

Security, risk, and platform leads who need enough detail to implement the baseline consistently.

## Priority Rule

The primary audience has priority. The memo should preserve a clear approval ask and move detailed implementation mechanics into follow-up work.

## Reader Questions

- What decision is requested?
- Which public sources support the baseline?
- Which controls are minimum requirements?
- What does the paper avoid claiming?
- What changes for pilot teams immediately?
