## Synthesis

The sources support a minimum public-source baseline for internal generative AI pilots. They do not prove that a specific internal baseline will reduce incidents, accelerate approvals, or satisfy every regulatory obligation.

## Draft Guidance

- Cite source IDs beside material framework claims.
- Keep the recommendation to a minimum pilot baseline.
- Avoid saying the public sources guarantee secure pilots.
