# Audience Profile

This profile defines who the piece is for and how the writing should adapt to them.

## Audience Source

- **Mode:** Single.
- **Curated personas used:** None.
- **Custom audience defined:** Yes.
- **Persona improvements made before use:** Added delivery-status expectations and sponsor-action needs.
- **Primary audience priority:** Delivery sponsor and immediate platform stakeholders.

## Audience Set

Use this section when the paper has multiple audiences.

| Audience | Priority | Why They Matter | Success Condition |
|----------|----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Delivery sponsor and platform stakeholders | Primary | They need a fast status read and clear action requests. | They know current state, blocker, owner, and next checkpoint. |

## Reader

- **Primary audience:** Internal delivery sponsor and platform stakeholders.
- **Role/context:** People tracking near-term platform delivery progress.
- **Expertise level:** Familiar with the project context.
- **Time/patience:** Low; they need the update in under two minutes.

## Current Beliefs

They likely believe:

- The work is broadly on track.
- The remaining risk is dependency coordination rather than strategy.
- They need to know whether their help is required this week.

## Needs

They need:

- Current status.
- What changed since the last update.
- Blockers and owners.
- Specific asks.
- Next checkpoint.

## Objections

They may object that:

- The update is too vague to act on.
- The blocker is named without an owner.
- The next checkpoint is unclear.

## Desired Shift

After reading, they should:

- **Understand:** The work is progressing but one dependency needs attention.
- **Believe:** The next action is clear and manageable.
- **Do:** Confirm the dependency owner and attend the next checkpoint if needed.

## Adaptation Rules

- **Context depth:** Low.
- **Technical depth:** Low to moderate.
- **Proof standard:** Current delivery-state evidence, not external sourcing.
- **Tone fit:** Direct, brief, and operational.
- **Conflict rule:** Action clarity beats background detail.

## Scoring Emphasis

- Status clarity.
- Specific next action.
- Blocker ownership.
- Brevity.
- No unnecessary evidence burden.
